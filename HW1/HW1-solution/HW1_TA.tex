\section{(c) Discussion}\label{c-discussion}

Maximum Likelihood estimation (MLE) and Least squares estimation (LSE)
were conducted to estimate the parameters of the seven models. Based on
goodness-of-fit (GOF) indices, the model with higher log-likelihood,
lower SSE (sum of squared errors), or higher \(R^2\) is preferred as the
better model. According to the results, POW2 model and EXPOW models are
nested and both showed the best fit. However, the simpler model (with
fewer parameters) should be chosen as the best model when GOF is same.
Therefore we can conclude that POW2 is the best model.
